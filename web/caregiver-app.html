How Stroke Recovery Cards Work

What are Stroke Recovery Cards?

Stroke Recovery Cards help patients regain strength and independence through daily, personalized exercises. Each card is designed for patients with vision or cognitive challenges — large text, colorblind-safe colors, and clear step-by-step instructions. No medical training required.

🎯 The Four Exercise Categories

★ Hand Exercises (Blue)

Improve grip and finger dexterity with squeezes and pinches

● Shoulder Exercises (Orange)

Increase range of motion with arm lifts and circles

■ Arm Exercises (Green)

Strengthen arms and improve coordination

▲ Leg Exercises (Purple)

Build leg strength and mobility

🖨️ Output Formats

4 on 1 Page (Quadrant)

All four exercise categories on a single A4 sheet. Great for caregivers to reference multiple exercises at once.

Large Cards (2x Size)

One exercise per page, double size. Better for patients with low vision or cognitive limitations.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Laminate printed sheets for durability
  • Use whiteboard markers on laminated sheets to check off daily completions
  • Print in color for best readability
  • Keep sheets visible in the patient's exercise area
